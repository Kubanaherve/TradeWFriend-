# TradeWFriend+ - Quick Start Guide

## 🎯 Get Started in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Launch & Login
1. Open the app
2. Enter your phone number
3. Create/enter your 6-digit PIN
4. First-time users: Create account, give it a name

### Step 2: Configure (Settings)
1. Tap the **Settings ⚙️** button in the menu
2. Enter your **Business Name** (e.g., "Mohamed's Shop")
3. Enter your **Starting Money** (your initial capital)
4. Enter your **Target Money** (your goal amount, optional)
5. Tap **Save Settings** ✓

### Step 3: Start Trading
1. Go back to Dashboard
2. Choose what to do:
   - 📝 **Add Debt** - Record customer purchases
   - 📋 **Debts** - See who owes you money
   - 📈 **Sales** - Track what you sold
   - 📦 **Inventory** - Manage your stock
   - 👥 **Customers** - View customer list
   - 📬 **Inbox** - Get debt alerts

## 🔄 Factory Reset

### When to Use
- Starting fresh with new business
- Cleaning up old test data
- Preparing for new year
- Sharing the app with someone else

### What Gets Deleted ❌
- All customers
- All sales records
- All inventory items
- All cached data

### What's Preserved ✅
- Your business name
- Your starting capital
- Your target goal
- Your PIN login

### How to Do It
1. Open **Settings ⚙️**
2. Scroll down to "Danger Zone"
3. Tap **"Factory Reset All Data"**
4. Confirm the warning
5. Data is cleared, you're ready to start fresh!

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: What if I forget my PIN?**
A: You'll need to create a new account (old data will remain).

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes! Export debts as PDF from the Debts page.

**Q: Does this work offline?**
A: Yes! Everything works without internet.

**Q: How do I back up my data?**
A: Data is auto-saved. Set up Supabase for cloud backup.

**Q: Can I use multiple accounts?**
A: Yes! Switch accounts on the login page.

**Q: Can I change my business name?**
A: Yes! Go to Settings anytime.

**Q: What happens to data after reset?**
A: It's permanently deleted (except settings).

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### App Won't Start?
1. Clear app cache
2. Restart phone
3. Try again
4. If still broken: Reinstall app

### Lost Data?
1. Check if you logged in to same account
2. Try refreshing
3. Check localStorage isn't cleared
4. Consider factory reset if corrupted

### Slow Performance?
1. Close other apps
2. Clear cache
3. Restart phone
4. Do factory reset to clean up

### Can't Login?
1. Check PIN is correct
2. Try creating new account
3. Ensure strong internet
4. Clear app data & reinstall
